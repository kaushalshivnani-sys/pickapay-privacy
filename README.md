Privacy Policy for PickAPay

Last updated: May 2026

PickAPay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting 
your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle 
information when you use our mobile application PickAPay.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
PickAPay does not collect any sensitive financial information.
We do not ask for credit card numbers, CVV, expiry dates, 
PINs, or any banking credentials.

The only information we store is:
- Your selected bank names and card types (e.g. HDFC Credit)
- Your card variant names (e.g. Millennia, Regalia)
- Your savings log entries that you manually create
- A randomly generated anonymous user ID

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or location.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
The information stored is used solely to show you relevant 
credit card offers within the app. We do not sell, share, 
or transfer your data to any third parties.

DATA STORAGE
Your wallet and savings data is stored securely in our 
cloud database hosted on Render.com. We use industry 
standard security practices to protect your data.

COMMUNITY SUBMISSIONS
If you choose to submit an offer, the offer details you 
provide are stored in our database for review. No personal 
information is collected with submissions.

CHILDREN
PickAPay is not intended for use by children under 13 years 
of age.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will 
notify users of any significant changes by updating the date 
at the top of this page.

CONTACT US
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please 
contact us at: pickapayapp@gmail.com

This privacy policy is effective as of May 2026.
